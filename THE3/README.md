## Description

This assignment focuses on several advanced topics from discrete mathematics, including number theory, combinatorics, symmetric polynomials, and group symmetries.
The problems require formal mathematical proofs and reasoning.

## Topics Covered

* Divisibility and number theory
* Mathematical induction
* Symmetric polynomials
* Multisets and combinatorics
* Symmetries of regular polygons
* Permutations and Rubik’s Cube transformations

## Problem Overview

### Question 1 – Divisibility and Number Theory

Proof-based questions related to divisibility, modular arithmetic, and combinatorial identities.

### Question 2 – Inductive Proofs and Symmetric Polynomials

Problems involving mathematical induction and relationships between symmetric polynomials.

### Question 3 – Multisets and Combinatorics

Counting problems involving multisets and distributions of objects.

### Question 4 – Symmetries of a Regular Polygon

Questions about symmetry operations of an n-gon and their algebraic structure.

### Question 5 – Rubik’s Cube Permutations

Analysis of cube twists using permutations and reasoning about properties of these transformations.
